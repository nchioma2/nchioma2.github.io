Design Focus

Making Entertainment Even More Enjoyable.

Netflix.com has been a widely used platform for virtual entertainment, especially during this era of quarentine. It's easy-to-use features allows you to select TV shows, movies, and much more at the tip of your finger. With the multidude of Netflix viewing platforms, this site will discuss design issues among it's web-based source. The clients and audience include everyone who watches TV shows and movies from online sources. Continue for more information.

When asked, participants said they enjoyed the:

  • Accessibilty of Movies and TV Shows
  • New Show Recommendations
  • New Profile Feature

User Personas

Osinachi N

Avid Netflix Binger

Osi is a high school student (age 17) currently living in a period of quarantine. With the COVID-19 season, she enjoys binge-watching Netflix shows and considers herself a pro when it comes to selecting shows. She is a Nigerian-American and loves watching international shows such as K-Dramas, Nollywood films, and much more. She loves rewatching shows on her free time and is based locally in West Palm Beach, Fl.

Task 1

Where do you go when you first open the app?

Task 2

Demonstrate how you would find shows you've finished watching.

User Reaction for Task 1

User scrolled through "My List" from Home searching for history. User mentions that "My List" sometimes contains movies they have saved if they planned on watching later or movies they really enjoyed when watching. User then decides to look for internaltional movies, speficially Nigerian films. User does not see any and settles on a film. When user clicks on online version of the film, the movie starts automatically without a trailer or summary or rating or length of series. There is also a top 10 US Shows but not International Films.

User Reaction for Task 2

User scrolled clicked on "My List" tab searching for history. User then realized the "My List" only contains movies that they had added themselves. The "My List" appears unorganized and cluttered.

Task 3

Could you demonstrate a TV Show that has new episodes?

Task 4

How would you and a grousp of friends pick a movie to watch on Netflix?

Task 5

How do you choose a new show to watch?

User Reaction for Task 3

User found a show with new updates. Although the show was also mentioned in "Trending", user clicked on the show that was in "My List". User also mentioned that she had watched the show before but wanted to keep up with new episodes when they came out.

User Reaction for Task 4

User mentions that they usually watch trending movies or movies that have been spoken/recommended by other people. If no such movie exists, they usually choose a genre that all of them enjoy and pick a movie from there. The comedies section is unorganized and does not provide much insight on making a choice.
